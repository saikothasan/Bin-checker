FAQ

What is a BIN?
A BIN (Bank Identification Number) is the first six digits of a credit or debit card number and it identifies the institution that issued the card.

How accurate is the BIN data?
We strive to keep our data as accurate and up-to-date as possible, but there may be occasional discrepancies.
